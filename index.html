Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and one of the finest batsmen in modern-day cricket. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Kohli has established himself as a dominant force in international cricket. He is known for his aggressive batting style, consistency, and exceptional leadership skills.

He was the captain of the Indian national team across all formats and has led India to numerous victories. Under his leadership, India achieved significant milestones, including a historic Test series win in Australia. Kohli has numerous records to his name, including being one of the fastest players to score 8,000, 9,000, and 10,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs).

Beyond cricket, Virat Kohli is known for his fitness regime, brand endorsements, and philanthropy. He is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, and the couple is widely admired by fans worldwide.
